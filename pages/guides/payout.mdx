# Payouts

Your balance is always in USD, regardless of your location. When we pay you out,
we'll convert the amount to your local currency using the current exchange rate.
However, if you're not in a region where we support local transfers, we'll have
to wire transfer the funds to you, which typically takes 2-5 business days and
incurs a $10 fee.

Payouts are handled through Stripe Connect if you've integrated it. But even if
you haven't, we'll still process your PayPal and crypto payments through bank
wire or local transfers.

If you haven't set up Stripe Connect, we'll send your payouts to your bank
account. Your information is safe with a third-party payout company that ensures
compliance with KYC/AML regulations.

Below is the fee structure for payout processing. Please note that the cutoff
time for payout requests is 7:00 PM UTC.
